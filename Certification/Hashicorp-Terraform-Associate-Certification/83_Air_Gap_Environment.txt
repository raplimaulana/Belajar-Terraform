### AIR GAP ENVIRONMENT ###
What is air gap?
Air gap or disconnected network is a network security measure employed on one or more computers to ensure that a secure computer network is physically isolated from unsecure network e.g. public internet.
In otherwise, no internet, no outside activity.

Indsutries in public sector (e.g. governemt, military) or large enterprise e.g. finance and energy often employ air gap network.
Hashicorp terraform enterprise supports an installation type for air gapped environments.
To install or update terraform enterprise, you will supply an "air gap bundle" which is an archive of a terraform enterprise release version.

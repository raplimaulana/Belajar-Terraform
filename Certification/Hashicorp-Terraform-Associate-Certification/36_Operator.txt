### OPERATOR ###
Operators are mathematical operations you can preform to numbers within expressions.
- Multiplication 
  a*b 

- Division
  a/b

- Modulus
  a%b

- Addition
  a+b

- Substraction
  a-b

- Flip to negative (*-1)
  -a

- Equals
  a==b

- Does not equals
  a!=b

- Less than
  a<b

- Greater then
  a>b

- Less then or equal
  a<=b

- Greater then or equal
  a>=b

- or
  a||b

- and
  a&&b

- Flip boolean
  !a
